The Craft

01

Sourcing the Bean

Single-origin cacao from Ecuador, Ghana & Vietnam. Hand-selected for exceptional flavor profiles.

02

Fermentation & Drying

5–7 days of sun-drying. This crucial step develops the complex precursor flavors.

03

Roasting

Slow-roasted at 140°C to bring out the depth and richness of the cocoa.

04

Grinding & Conching

72-hour conch for an unbelievably silky texture that melts perfectly on the palate.

05

Tempering

Precise crystallization for the signature glossy finish and satisfying snap.

06

Moulding & Finishing

Hand-finished and foil-wrapped to preserve freshness and ensure a premium unboxing experience.
